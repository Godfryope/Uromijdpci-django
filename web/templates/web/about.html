Who we are

A Catholic Non-Profit making and Humanitarian Organization.

Uromi JDPCI is a Faith-based Non-Governmental Organization borne out of the Catholic Diocese of Uromi with a mandate to promote Justice and Peace, encourage meaningful development and to provide succor for the less privileged. Our activities cover the geographical area of the Catholic Diocese of Uromi which comprises the 5 Local Government Areas in Edo Central Senatorial district.
